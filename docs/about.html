About Teeth Fix

Teeth Fix is an iOS app that helps you visualize dental-style enhancements on your own photos using artificial intelligence. Upload a selfie or portrait, choose a treatment type (alignment, braces removal, whitening, or an all-in-one enhancement), and get a photorealistic preview—without replacing professional dental care.

🎯 How It Works

Teeth Fix keeps the flow simple:

  1. Upload a photo: From your library or camera (selfie or portrait works)
  2. Pick a treatment: Alignment, remove braces, whitening, or all
  3. Generate: AI applies your chosen enhancement
  4. Unlock result: Use credits to view your finished image
  5. Save & share: Export to Photos or share
